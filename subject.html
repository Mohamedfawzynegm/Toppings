My favourite Subject is Medicine

Medicine includes many fields like:

Anatomy: study of body structure.

Physiology: study of body functions.

Biochemistry: study of chemical processes in the body.

Pathology: study of disease causes and effects.

Pharmacology: study of drugs and their effects.

Microbiology: study of microorganisms and infection.

Immunology: study of the immune system.

Genetics: study of heredity and inherited diseases.
